WABI-TV, virtual channel 5 (VHF digital channel 13), is a dual CBS/CW+-affiliated television station licensed to Bangor, Maine, United States, serving Central and Eastern Maine. The station is owned by Atlanta-based Gray Television. WABI-TV's studios are located on Hildreth Street in West Bangor, and its transmitter is located on Peaked Mountain in Dixmont.
Prior to 2017, WABI-TV was the flagship station of its founding owner Diversified Communications, which was owned by the Hildreth family of Bangor.
