Herman van den Belt (born 1 January 1970) is a Dutch professional basketball coach, currently for Landstede Hammers of the Dutch Basketball League (DBL).Since 1996, van den Belt has been the head coach of Landstede Zwolle. The only exception was the 2009–10 season, in which he was an assistant-coach of West-Brabant Giants. In 2019, he won his first DBL championship with Zwolle.
