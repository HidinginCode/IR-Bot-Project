Comrades is an American rock band from Richmond, Virginia. The band started making music in 2008 and released a studio album, Safekeeper, in 2014 via Blood and Ink Records.Their members are husband and wife, Joe, a guitarist, and Laura McElroy, a vocalist and bassist. Unclean vocalist and drummer Ben Trussell announced in June 2017 that he has decided to amicably part ways with the band and work on a solo musical project called BJORN. Currently, former Life in Your Way and Conveyer drummer John Gaskill is filling in.
