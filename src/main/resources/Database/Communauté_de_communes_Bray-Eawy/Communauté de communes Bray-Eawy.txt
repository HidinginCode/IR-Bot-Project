The Communauté de communes Bray-Eawy is a communauté de communes in the Seine-Maritime département and in the Normandy région of France. It was formed on 1 January 2017 by the merger of the former Communauté de communes du Pays Neufchâtelois, Communauté de communes de Saint-Saëns-Portes de Bray and 8 communes from the former Communauté de communes du Bosc d'Eawy  on 1 January 2017. It consists of 46 communes, and its seat is in Neufchâtel-en-Bray.
