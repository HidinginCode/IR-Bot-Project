Joseph Aloysius Ward (September 7, 1884 –  August 11, 1934) was a Major League Baseball player who played second base for the Philadelphia Phillies in the 1906 season. He also played for the Phillies in 1909–1910 seasons and also part of 1909 with the New York Highlanders. In 166 games, Ward had 110 hits in 465 at-bats, for a .237 batting average. He batted and threw right-handed. He was born and died in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
