The Academia Brasileira de Médicos Escritores (Brazilian Academy of Medical Writers)
Founded on November 26, 1987 and officially installed on May 26, 1989, in the city of Rio de Janeiro the academy brings together medical writers in Brazil.
It is the first academy in Brazil, and the world, that uniquely brings together medical writers.
It is also affiliated with the Association of Brazilian Academies of Arts (Associação das Academias de Letras do Brasil).
